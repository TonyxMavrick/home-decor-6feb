Our Story

Founded in 2020, LuxeNest Decor emerged from a vision to merge cutting-edge technology with luxurious interior design. We saw a future where homes would be illuminated not just by light, but by innovation.

Our journey began in a small studio in New York, where our founders experimented with LED technology and smart home integration. Today, we've transformed over 500 spaces globally, bringing the cyberpunk aesthetic to modern living.
